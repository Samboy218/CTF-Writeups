Fare Type Information

Bulk Fare on Charter Operated Flights: We offer special "bulk" fares on our charter operated flights. Bulk airfares are special designated fares to save you money and offer the most flexibility in revising your vacation itinerary.

Bulk Fare on Scheduled Airlines: We offer special "bulk" designated fares with name-brand airlines that can save you money and are the most flexible when revising your itinerary. Each scheduled airline has unique cancellation and revision penalties on bulk fares ranging from $50-$200 per ticket or in some cases nonrefundable fares may apply. You may use your scheduled commercial airline tickets for up to one year when making a revision to the date of travel for the cost of the revision fee plus any fare increases. The Penalty Waiver option does not cover scheduled air commercial airline ticketing or cancellation fees. To see details of bulk fare penalties by scheduled commercial airline see our Bill of Rights.

Published Fare on Scheduled Airlines: We shop name brand airlines to offer you the lowest priced published fares at the time of purchase. These fares have restrictions if revisions are needed. Published Fares purchased on scheduled commercial airlines are nonrefundable if canceled. You may use your commercial airline published tickets for up to one year when making a revision to the date of travel for the cost of the revision fee plus any fare increases. The Penalty Waiver does not cover scheduled commercial airline ticketing or cancellation fees.
